Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that everyone in our school is treated equally. We also want to provide services so people who are being bullied can report these instances so we can look into them further and potentially take action.

This is some of the resources we offer:

  • Counseling
  • Annonymous Reports
  • Meeting Scheduling
